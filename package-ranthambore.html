Description

Ranthambhore Heritage is an ideal spot for holidaymakers who wish to spend some quality time away from the hustle and bustle of city life in Ranthambhore,Rajasthan.Ranthambore National Park is one of the most popular national parks in India and is world famous as one of the best places in the world to see a tiger in the wild.The beauty of the wildlife is superb and seeing them in close proximity gives a thrilling experience to travellers here.The property is ocated at the distance of (11.7 km) from Bus Stand,(7.8 km) from Sawai Madhopur railway station and One can opt for state transport or can hire private vehicle to reach the hotel. The rooms in the hotel are well-maintained, ventilated and clean, and come with good quality furniture. Depending upon their needs, guests can choose from Air-conditioned and Non Air-conditioned rooms.Snuggle up in our custom made cozy bedding.Essential amenities like Wi-Fi, telephone, hot and cold water,Swimming Pool, essential toiletries and many more are been offered at the hotel.In case of medical emergencies, guests can request for doctor on call facility.Guests are served with some exotic and freshly prepared delicacies at the in-house restaurant. Experience a grand stay and a warm hospitality at Ranthambhore Heritage!
