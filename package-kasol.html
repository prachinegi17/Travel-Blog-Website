Description

Conveniently located in Manikaran, Magnum Camps Kasol is a great base from which to explore this vibrant city. From here, guests can enjoy easy access to all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

The facilities and services provided by Magnum Camps Kasol ensure a pleasant stay for guests. Guests of the hotel can enjoy on-site features like 24-hour security, daily housekeeping, taxi service, ticket service, 24-hour front desk.

The hotel features 12 beautifully appointed guest rooms, many of which include carpeting, mirror, private entrance, umbrella, wake-up service. The hotel offers an excellent variety of recreational facilities, including badminton court, garden. Discover an engaging blend of professional service and a wide array of features at Magnum Camps Kasol.
